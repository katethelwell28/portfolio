about me

Working in software, I’ve realised that technology is leading our world so I am intrigued to learn more about this fascinating and growing industry.

What started as a lockdown project, freeCodeCamp was my first introduction into the world of coding.

Having enjoyed learning the inns and outs of HTML and CSS, I felt confident to apply to a 2 month ‘Introduction To Web Development’ course in June 2021 with the amazing Code First Girls, which was sponsored by NatWest. During this course, we had weekly online sessions with other females and our final project was a website developed within our groups.

I truly found a passion for Web Development after successfully completely the course, and I decided to enrol on a Front-End Engineer career path course with Codecademy in September 2021. I am currently 33% of the way through and this personal portfolio is my first big project, putting everything I have learnt into practice!
